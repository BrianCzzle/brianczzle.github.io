Green Jobs In Sustainable Transport

In Electric Vehicles

ABSTRACT

The rise of electric vehicles(EVs) is crucial for reducing carbon emissions and promoting sustainable. This project explores the role of gren jobs in the EV industry, focusing on manufacturing, battery technology, charging infrasture, and policy development. These jobs are key to advancing clean transportation and supporting broader environmental goals.

INTRODUCTION

As the world moves toward cleaner transportation, electric vehicles(EVs) are at the forefront of reducing carbon emissions. Alongside this shift, green jobs in the EV sector are rapidly manufacturing, battery innovation and charging infrasture. These roles are essential not only for environmental progress but also for fostering economic growth and technological advancements. This project will examine the significance of green jobs in the EV industry and how they contribute to both sustainability and economic development. By exploring key areas such as manufacturing, infrastructure, and policy, this research highlights the importance of these roles in driving the future of clean transportation.
